PetReunite — App Feature Summary
==================================

WHAT IS IT?
A mobile app to help reunite lost pets with their owners across Australia. Available on iPhone and Android.


CORE FEATURES:
- Report a lost or found pet with photos (up to 5), location, breed, size, colour, and markings
- Home feed showing all lost and found pet reports from all users
- Interactive map with colour-coded pins for lost pets, found pets, vets, and shelters
- Register your pets with microchip numbers, medical notes, and biometric ID photos (nose, eyes, face close-ups)
- One-tap "Report as Lost" from a registered pet profile
- "My Pets" tab to manage all your profiles and reports in one place


AI-POWERED FEATURES (Premium):
- AI Matching: Automatically finds potential matches between lost and found reports using photo comparison and text analysis
- Scan Online Posts: Paste text from Facebook or community boards, the AI extracts pet details and finds matches in the app
- Quick Snap: Take a photo of a pet you've spotted and instantly match it against all registered pets


COMMUNITY FEATURES:
- Community Tips: Leave comments and helpful info on any report
- Timeline: See a chronological history of updates on each report
- Social sharing to Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp
- Likes and support on reports
- Reward Pool: Community members can contribute to a reward for finding a lost pet
- Boost Report: Increase visibility in the feed
- Happy Tails: Read reunion success stories
- Printable missing pet flyer generator
- Area alerts for nearby lost/found pets


OTHER FEATURES:
- Suburb Directory: Browse registered pets by suburb (privacy-safe)
- Vet and Shelter Directory with map markers
- Safety Tips Guide
- How It Works guide and FAQ
- Referral Program: Invite friends and earn free premium days
- Social Ambassador: Share on social media to earn premium rewards


PRICING:
- Free: Limited reports and profiles
- Premium: $4.99/month or $49.99/year
  Unlocks: AI matching, scan posts, multi-photo upload, biometric scanning, Quick Snap, and boost


SECURITY:
- Secure accounts with encrypted passwords
- Australian Privacy Act 1988 compliant
- Users control their own data (edit, delete anytime)
- Block and report other users
