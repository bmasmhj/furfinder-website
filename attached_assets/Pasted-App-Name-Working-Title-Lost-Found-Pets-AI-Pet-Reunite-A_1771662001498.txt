App Name (Working Title):
Lost & Found Pets – AI Pet Reunite App
App Concept:
A mobile application designed to help reunite lost pets with their owners using community reporting, location tracking, and AI-powered photo matching technology. Pet owners can register their pets, upload photos and details, and instantly receive alerts if a similar pet is found nearby.
Target Users:
Pet owners, animal lovers, veterinarians, shelters, councils, and community members.
Primary Goal:
Create a trusted community platform where lost pets can be quickly identified and returned safely to their owners through automated matching and real-time notifications.
Core Features (MVP – Phase 1):
User Accounts:
Secure registration and login
Profile management
Pet Profiles:
Upload multiple pet photos
Pet name, breed, size, colour, markings
Microchip number (optional)
Medical notes (optional)
Owner contact details
Lost Pet Reporting:
Mark pet as LOST
Last seen location
Date and time
Reward option (optional)
Found Pet Reporting:
Upload photo of found pet
Automatic GPS/location tagging
Description notes
Search and Discovery:
Browse lost and found pets nearby
Filter by suburb or radius
Map-based viewing
Notifications:
Alerts when a similar pet is reported nearby
Status updates for listings
Communication:
Secure in-app messaging or contact system between finder and owner
Admin Panel:
Content moderation
User management
Reporting and abuse prevention
Phase 2 Features (Advanced):
AI Image Matching:
Compare uploaded photos of found pets with lost pet database
Identify breed, colour patterns, and physical features
Provide match confidence score
Smart Alerts:
Automatic owner notification when high-probability matches occur
Community Integration:
Animal shelters and veterinary clinic accounts
Council and rescue organisation participation
Safety and Privacy:
Data privacy controls
Verified accounts
Photo moderation system
Technology Requirements:
Cross-platform mobile app (iOS and Android)
Cloud database and secure photo storage
GPS location services
Push notifications
Scalable backend system
Future Expansion:
Nationwide launch in Australia followed by global expansion
Multi-language support
Reward/donation system
Social sharing features
Desired Outcome:
A simple, reliable, and widely adopted platform that increases the chances of safely reuniting lost pets with their families quickly and efficiently.
