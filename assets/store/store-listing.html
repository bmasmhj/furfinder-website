Full Description
PetReunite is Australia's dedicated lost and found pet platform, designed to reunite lost pets with their families faster than ever before. HOW IT WORKS 1. Report a lost or found pet with photos, location, and details 2. Our AI instantly scans and matches reports across the community 3. Get real-time area alerts when pets are reported near you 4. Connect with the pet's owner and bring them home KEY FEATURES AI-Powered Matching Our advanced AI compares photos, breeds, colours, markings, and location to find potential matches between lost and found reports. Simply upload a photo and let our technology do the work. Biometric ID Scanning Register close-up photos of your pet's nose, eyes, and face for enhanced identification. Use Quick Snap to match a spotted pet against registered biometric scans. Interactive Map View lost and found pets on an Australia-wide map. Find nearby vets, shelters, and rescue organisations from our directory of 89+ listings across every state and territory. Pet Profile Registration Register your pets before they go missing. Store photos, microchip numbers, medical notes, and biometric scans so you're prepared if the worst happens. Quickly report a registered pet as lost with one tap. Area Alerts Get notified instantly when a pet is reported lost or found within your chosen radius. Never miss a sighting in your neighbourhood. Community Tips Leave comments and tips on reports to help reunite pets. Share sightings, suggest locations, and support fellow pet owners. Printable Flyers Generate professional lost pet flyers directly from your report. Download and print to post in your local area. Happy Tails Read heartwarming reunion stories from pets that have been successfully reunited with their families through PetReunite. Reward Pool Create a community reward pool for lost pets to incentivise finders and increase the chances of a safe return. Boost Reports Increase the visibility of your report to reach more people in your area when time is critical. PREMIUM FEATURES ($4.99/month or $49.99/year) - Unlimited pet reports and profiles - AI-powered photo matching - Scan online posts from social media - Multi-photo uploads (up to 5 photos) - Biometric ID scanning - Priority area alerts PRIVACY & SAFETY - Compliant with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 - Comment moderation with profanity and spam filtering - Block and report users - Full account deletion available - Your data stored securely on Australian-compliant servers PetReunite is built for the Australian community. Whether you've lost a pet, found a stray, or want to be prepared, PetReunite gives you the tools and community to help bring pets home. Download now and join thousands of Australians helping reunite pets with their families.
